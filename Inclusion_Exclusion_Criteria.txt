Inclusion and Exclusion Criteria
--------------------------------

Inclusion Criteria:
1. Peer-reviewed journal articles.
2. Published between 2020 and 2025.
3. Written in English.
4. Focus on gamification or game-based learning in educational contexts.
5. Explicit discussion of cognitive outcomes (e.g., critical thinking, cognitive engagement, problem solving).
6. Empirical studies, systematic reviews, or well-founded conceptual/theoretical papers.
7. Studies involving AI, adaptive systems, learning analytics, or emerging educational technologies.

Exclusion Criteria:
1. Conference abstracts, posters, editorials, book reviews, or opinion pieces.
2. Studies not related to education or learning contexts.
3. Papers focusing solely on entertainment games without educational intent.
4. Studies reporting only motivational or affective outcomes without cognitive dimensions.
5. Non-English publications.
6. Duplicate records across databases.
7. Papers without accessible full text.

Application of criteria:
Inclusion and exclusion criteria were applied during title/abstract screening and full-text review stages by the author. Disagreements or ambiguities were resolved through iterative reassessment.
